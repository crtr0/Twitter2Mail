Twitter2Mail
alpha v0.2 - your mileage may vary!

Written by Carter Rabasa [web] [ff] [t]

Twitter2Mail is a simple app that monitors search.twitter.com and emails you your replies. Simply enter your Twitter username and email address. Once your information is saved any new replies will be emailed to you every 15 minutes. Curious about what makes us different from every other @reply emailer?

  • Digest and non-digest modes. Non-digest mode will send super-concise emails for each @reply. Get these via text message by inputing your mobile email number.
  • Links to view threads in digest mode emails. Great for seeing what someone was replying to!
